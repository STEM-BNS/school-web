How to Be a Student at STEM Bani Sweif School

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • High grades in Science and Mathematics
  • Pass the entrance exam
  • English language proficiency

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous academic records
  • Recent photographs
  • Application form

Application Process

1

Academic Requirements

Get 98% in Grade 9 or 95% with full marks in one of these subjects:

  • English
  • Science
  • Mathematics
2

Online Application

Submit your online application to secure a seat for the acceptance exam

3

Acceptance Exams

Take the following exams:

  • IQ Test
  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Science
4

Results

Check your acceptance status on the official Ministry of Education website
