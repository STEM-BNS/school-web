Capstone Projects

Innovative Solutions for Real-World Challenges

Engineering Design Process

Our capstone projects follow a rigorous Engineering Design Process (EDP) that enables students to develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

01

Ask & Define

Identify and research the problem, gathering information and defining project constraints and requirements.

02

Imagine & Brainstorm

Generate multiple creative solutions and explore different approaches to solve the problem.

03

Plan & Design

Select the best solution and create detailed plans, including drawings, models, and specifications.

04

Create & Build

Construct a prototype or model following the design specifications and project requirements.

05

Test & Evaluate

Test the prototype, collect data, and evaluate its performance against the design criteria.

06

Improve & Optimize

Refine and improve the design based on test results and feedback, iterating as needed.

Project Development Process

Research & Planning

Identify problems and develop project proposals with faculty guidance.

Design & Development

Create prototypes and implement solutions using appropriate technologies.

Testing & Refinement

Conduct experiments, gather data, and improve the solution.

Presentation & Evaluation

Present findings to faculty and industry experts for feedback.
